What is Perception Lab?

Perception Lab is an interactive 3D platform where you can visually experience the diversity of perspectives. Through the symbol of glasses, you can intuitively understand how different viewpoints interact, merge, and sometimes collide.

üéØ Core Concept

We all see the world through different "glasses." This project visualizes the moments when these diverse perspectives meet in beautiful and meaningful ways. With a single click, experience perspectives separating, merging, and distorting in real-time.

Built with Three.js advanced 3D graphics and GSAP animations, it goes beyond mere visuals to provide philosophical insights.
